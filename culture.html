Compliance

Regulatory Compliance

We are committed to full compliance with international regulations and platform policies.

GDPR Compliance

Full compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation for European users, including data access rights, consent management, and the right to be forgotten.

CCPA Compliance

Adherence to the California Consumer Privacy Act, providing California residents with rights over their personal information and transparent data practices.

COPPA Compliance

Strict adherence to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, ensuring age-appropriate content and parental controls for users under 13.

App Store Guidelines

Complete compliance with Google Play Developer Program Policies and Apple Developer Program License Agreement for app distribution.

Age Ratings

Proper age rating classification for all applications on app stores, ensuring age-appropriate content and advertising across all platforms.

Regional Policies

Compliance with regional data protection laws including PIPL (China), LGPD (Brazil), POPIA (South Africa), and other international regulations.
