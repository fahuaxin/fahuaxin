Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Zhengzhou Fahuaxin Network Technology Co., Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications, website, and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including but not limited to: name, email address, phone number, postal address, payment information, and any other information you provide when using our services or contacting us.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our mobile applications or website, we may automatically collect: device identifiers, operating system information, browser type, IP address, usage data, app performance metrics, and location data (with your consent).

3. Use of Information

We use collected information to: provide and maintain our services, process transactions, send you updates and promotional materials, improve our products and services, comply with legal obligations, and protect our rights and prevent fraud.

4. Third-Party Services and Ad Networks

Our mobile applications may integrate third-party advertising services and analytics platforms to support our business operations. These services may include:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements in our mobile applications
  • Facebook Audience Network: For targeted advertising
  • Unity Ads: For game and app advertising
  • AppLovin: For mobile advertising and monetization
  • IronSource: For mobile app advertising and monetization
  • MoPub (Twitter): For mobile ad serving
  • Amazon Ads: For display and video advertising
  • Microsoft Advertising: For search and display advertising
  • Pangle: For mobile video advertising
  • Mintegral: For mobile advertising and analytics
  • Chartboost: For mobile game advertising
  • Vungle (Digital Turbine): For in-app video advertising
  • AdColony: For mobile video advertising
  • InMobi: For mobile advertising and monetization
  • Tapjoy: For mobile advertising and engagement
  • StartApp: For mobile advertising
  • Facebook Analytics: For app usage analytics
  • Firebase (Google): For analytics and crash reporting
  • AppsFlyer: For mobile attribution and analytics
  • Adjust: For mobile measurement and attribution
  • Branch: For deep linking and attribution
  • Singular: For marketing analytics and attribution
  • Kochava: For mobile attribution and analytics

These third parties may collect information about your device, browsing activities, and usage patterns to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

5. Advertising and Ad Formats

Our mobile applications may display various advertising formats, including:

  • Splash Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed when opening the app
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Video advertisements that users can watch in exchange for in-app rewards
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed at natural transition points
  • Banner Ads: Standard display advertisements shown at the top or bottom of screens
  • Native Ads: Advertisements designed to match the visual style of the app content
  • Playable Ads: Interactive advertisements allowing users to preview app features

6. Data Sharing

We may share your information with: service providers who assist in our operations, advertising partners for ad targeting, legal authorities when required by law, business partners in case of mergers or acquisitions, and with your consent for other purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age (or the applicable age threshold in your jurisdiction, such as 16 for EU users under GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers, including standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions where applicable.

10. Regional Privacy Rights

10.1 European Union (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU, you have the right to: access your personal data, rectify inaccurate data, erase your data ("right to be forgotten"), restrict processing, data portability, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

10.2 California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to: know what personal information is collected, know how it is used and shared, delete personal information, opt-out of the sale of personal information, and non-discrimination for exercising rights.

10.3 Other Regions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where we operate, including but not limited to: PIPL (China), LGPD (Brazil), POPIA (South Africa), PDPA (Thailand), and other relevant data protection regulations.

11. App Store Compliance

Our mobile applications comply with the privacy requirements of major app distribution platforms, including:

  • Google Play: Adhering to Google Play's Developer Program Policies, including privacy disclosures and data safety requirements
  • Apple App Store: Following Apple's App Store Review Guidelines and providing accurate privacy nutrition labels
  • Amazon Appstore: Complying with Amazon's privacy and data handling requirements
  • Samsung Galaxy Store: Meeting Samsung's privacy and security standards
  • Huawei AppGallery: Following Huawei's privacy guidelines and regulations

12. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure servers, access controls, regular security assessments, and employee training on data protection.

13. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and applications may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information. You can control cookies through your browser settings or device preferences.

14. Your Choices

You may: opt-out of promotional communications, disable cookies in your browser, adjust ad tracking settings on your device, request access to or deletion of your data, and withdraw consent where applicable.

15. Device-Level Advertising Opt-Outs

You can opt out of targeted advertising through:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of personalized advertising
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads
  • Industry opt-out platforms such as the NAI (Network Advertising Initiative) and DAA (Digital Advertising Alliance)

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
